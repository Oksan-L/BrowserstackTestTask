## **Title**
005 - Swipe gesture verification						

## **Description**
Verify that swipe gestures work correctly in the swipe section of the app.

## **Preconditions**
- Android emulator or BrowserStack device is running  
- Appium server is running and connected  
- Android Native Demo App is installed on the device  

## **Test Steps**
1. Navigate to the swipe screen.
2. Perform a swipe gesture to the right.
3. Perform a swipe gesture to the left.

## **Expected Results**
1. Swipe screen is displayed.
2. Right swipe moves to the next element.
3. Left swipe moves to the previous element.

## **Linked Automation**
- **Test:** `SwipeScreen.test.js` 
- **Page:** `swipe.page.js`
- **Page:** `old-swipe.page.js`
