## **Test ID**
TC-004

## **Title**
Switch negative test

## **Description**
Verify that clicking the switch element toggles its state correctly.

## **Preconditions**
- Android emulator or BrowserStack device is running  
- Appium server is running and connected  
- Android Native Demo App is installed on the device  

## **Test Steps**
1. Click the ""Forms"" button and navigate to the forms screen
2. Check the initial state of the switch (on or off). 
3. Click the switch element.
4. Click the switch element again.

## **Expected Results**
1. Forms page is opened and displayed
2. Switch element is displayed and have t/f state
3. First click toggles the switch to the opposite state.
4. Second click toggles the switch back to the initial state.

## **Linked Automation**
- **Test:** `FormsScreen.test.js` 
- **Page:** `home.page.js`
- **Page:** `forms.page.js`
